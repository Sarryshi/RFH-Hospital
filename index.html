Ruai Family Hospital (RFH)

Ruai Family Hospital has over the years demystified maternal health. It is a low-cost facility focusing on lower and middle-income earners.Dr. Maxwel Okoth is the founder of Ruai Family Hospital. He graduated from University of Nairobi with a Bachelor of Medicine degree in 2010. He is one of the youngest doctors in Kenya to own and run a full-fledged medical facility.He started Ruai Family hospital at the age of 26 years just after finishing his medical school with a seed capital of 3000 USD.
